Who We Are

About Chandeshwari Arts

Serving institutions and individuals with high-grade digital banners, customized framing, photo studio sessions, and sublimation merchandise.

Crafting Memories & Professional Brands since 2012

For over a decade, Chandeshwari Arts (चण्डेश्वरी डिजिटल & फ्लेक्स प्रिन्ट) has been a trusted printing partner in Nepal. Our printing lab is built to solve one constant problem: clients need extremely high-definition details, durable materials, and quick turnaround times under one roof.

We specialized initially in flex hoardings and passport-size studio photography. Over the years, we expanded into sublimation transfers (T-shirts, caps, keyrings, lanyards), high-lock acrylic ID holders, customized condolence/baby-birth frames, and self-ink corporate stamps.

  • ✔

    Bulk Production Capabilities

    Equipped with commercial high-speed digital lamination and sublimation transfer presses to print thousands of ID cards or badges in days.

  • ✔

    True Color Match Technology

    Calibrated color spaces ensure that your institution's logo colors print accurately on lanyards, metal badges, and flex banners.
